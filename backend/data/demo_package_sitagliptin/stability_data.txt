STABILITY DATA SUMMARY — Sitagliptin Phosphate Monohydrate Tablets 100mg

Protocol Reference: CAM-STAB-SIT-001, Rev 2
ICH Q1A(R2) Compliant Study Design

STORAGE CONDITIONS STUDIED (CAMBREX BATCHES ONLY):

1. LONG-TERM STABILITY — 25°C / 60% RH
   Container: Al-Al blister (Cambrex packaging configuration, Alcan foil)
   Duration: 18 months complete
   Timepoints: Initial, 3M, 6M, 12M, 18M

   Results Summary:
   Timepoint | Assay (%) | Dissolution (%) | Chiral (S,%) | WC (%)  | XRPD Form
   Initial   |  99.7     |   91.2          |   < 0.05     |  3.92   | Monohydrate
   3 months  |  99.5     |   90.8          |   < 0.05     |  3.88   | Monohydrate
   6 months  |  99.3     |   90.4          |   < 0.05     |  3.85   | Monohydrate
   12 months |  99.1     |   89.9          |   < 0.05     |  3.82   | Monohydrate
   18 months |  98.9     |   89.5          |   < 0.05     |  3.80   | Monohydrate
   Status: PASSING all specifications at 18 months

2. ACCELERATED STABILITY — 40°C / 75% RH
   Container: Al-Al blister (Cambrex packaging configuration)
   Duration: 6 months complete

   Results Summary:
   Timepoint | Assay (%) | Dissolution (%) | WC (%)  | XRPD Form
   Initial   |  99.7     |   91.2          |  3.92   | Monohydrate
   3 months  |  99.2     |   88.4          |  3.62   | Monohydrate
   6 months  |  98.5     |   85.8          |  3.38   | Monohydrate (retained)
   Status: PASSING at 6 months — monohydrate form RETAINED at accelerated conditions

   NOTE: Water content decreased from 3.92% to 3.38% at 40°C/75% RH over 6 months.
   This approaches the lower limit of 3.5%. Al-Al blister provides adequate moisture barrier
   at Cambrex site — Recipharm WVTR equivalence for ACG foil not yet confirmed.

3. INTERMEDIATE STABILITY — 30°C / 65% RH
   Duration: 6 months
   Status: All parameters within specification — no anomalies

4. PHOTOSTABILITY — ICH Q1B
   Sitagliptin is LIGHT-SENSITIVE — open dish exposure showed 0.18% increase in related substances.
   Al-Al blister provides adequate light protection (wrapped condition: no significant change).
   NOTE: Light protection equivalence for Recipharm blister configuration not confirmed.

CRITICAL GAPS IN STABILITY PACKAGE:
- All data generated at Cambrex site — no Recipharm confirmatory batches initiated
- Water content downward trend at accelerated conditions — narrow spec (3.5–4.5%) is a concern
  if Recipharm WVTR is not equivalent to Cambrex Alcan blister foil
- XRPD polymorphic form testing: Recipharm cannot perform on-site; external lab plan absent
- 24-month long-term data not yet available for 24-month shelf life support
- Confirmatory batches at Recipharm: 0 of 3 required initiated

SHELF LIFE CLAIM: 24 months (based on Cambrex data; projected from 18-month data)
NOTE: Humidity control is the single most critical environmental variable for this product.
Recipharm facility qualification for humidity in all production and storage areas must be
reviewed before placing stability commitment batches.

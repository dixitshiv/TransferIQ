ANALYTICAL METHODS PACKAGE — Sitagliptin Phosphate Monohydrate Tablets 100mg

1. ASSAY — HPLC Method
   Reference: CAM-AM-SIT-001, Rev 4
   Instrument: Agilent 1260 Infinity HPLC
   Column: C18, 150 x 4.6mm, 3.5μm (Zorbax Eclipse Plus)
   Mobile phase: 10mM ammonium formate pH 3.5 : acetonitrile (60:40)
   Flow rate: 1.0 mL/min
   Detection: UV at 267nm
   System suitability: RSD NMT 0.5%, tailing NMT 2.0
   Validation: Fully validated per ICH Q2(R1)
   Method transfer status: COMPLETE — all equivalence criteria met at Recipharm site

2. CHIRAL PURITY — Chiral HPLC
   Reference: CAM-AM-SIT-002, Rev 2
   Instrument: Agilent 1260 with chiral column
   Column: Chiralpak IA, 250 x 4.6mm, 5μm
   Mobile phase: n-hexane : ethanol (70:30) + 0.1% TFA
   Flow rate: 1.0 mL/min
   Limit: (S)-enantiomer NMT 0.1%
   Validation: Validated (specificity, LOQ 0.02%, linearity 0.02–0.2%)
   Method transfer status: PARTIAL — column qualification complete;
   system suitability criteria (Rs NLT 2.0 between R and S peaks) not consistently met at Recipharm.
   NOTE: CRITICAL — Chiral purity is a regulatory requirement; method must be fully transferred
   before batch release. Chiralpak IA column lot-to-lot variability requires careful qualification.

3. POLYMORPHIC FORM — X-Ray Powder Diffraction (XRPD)
   Reference: CAM-AM-SIT-003, Rev 1
   Method: XRPD with Cu-Kα radiation
   Specification: Confirms monohydrate Form I via characteristic peaks at 2θ: 10.2°, 16.8°, 20.5°
   Validation: Specificity demonstrated (Form I vs. anhydrous Form II discrimination)
   Method transfer status: NOT STARTED
   NOTE: CRITICAL GAP — Recipharm Stockholm does not have on-site XRPD capability.
   External laboratory qualification required; sample chain-of-custody and turnaround time
   for batch release testing not addressed in this package.

4. DISSOLUTION — USP Apparatus II
   Reference: CAM-AM-SIT-004, Rev 2
   Medium: 900 mL pH 6.8 phosphate buffer (no surfactant)
   Speed: 50 rpm
   Temperature: 37.0 ± 0.5°C
   Timepoints: 15, 30 min; specification Q=80% at 30 min
   Method transfer status: COMPLETE — equivalence demonstrated

5. WATER CONTENT — Karl Fischer Titration
   Reference: CAM-AM-SIT-005, Rev 1
   Method: Volumetric KF
   Specification: 3.5–4.5% (narrow — confirms monohydrate stoichiometry)
   Validation: Complete
   Method transfer status: COMPLETE
   NOTE: Water content is used as a surrogate confirmation of polymorphic form retention.

6. RELATED SUBSTANCES — HPLC Gradient (ICH M7)
   Reference: CAM-AM-SIT-006, Rev 3
   Known impurities evaluated for mutagenic potential per ICH M7
   Acceptable daily intake limits applied per ICH M7 threshold of toxicological concern
   Method transfer status: PARTIAL — ICH M7 mutagenic impurity thresholds reviewed at Recipharm;
   confirmatory spiking study to demonstrate quantification at TTC level not yet performed.

STABILITY DATA SUMMARY — Ibuprofen Film-Coated Tablets 400mg

Protocol Reference: CAT-STAB-IBU-001, Rev 1
ICH Q1A(R2) Compliant Study Design

STORAGE CONDITIONS STUDIED (CATALENT BATCHES ONLY):

1. LONG-TERM STABILITY — 25°C / 60% RH
   Container: Al-Al blister (Catalent packaging configuration)
   Duration: 12 months complete
   Timepoints: Initial, 3M, 6M, 12M

   Results Summary:
   Timepoint | Assay (%) | Dissolution (%) | RS Total (%) | PSD d90 (μm)
   Initial   |  99.8     |   89.2          |   0.06       |   128
   3 months  |  99.6     |   88.9          |   0.07       |   ND
   6 months  |  99.4     |   88.1          |   0.08       |   ND
   12 months |  99.1     |   87.5          |   0.10       |   ND
   Status: PASSING all specifications at 12 months

2. ACCELERATED STABILITY — 40°C / 75% RH
   Container: Al-Al blister (Catalent packaging configuration)
   Duration: 6 months complete
   Timepoints: Initial, 3M, 6M

   Results Summary:
   Timepoint | Assay (%) | Dissolution (%) | RS Total (%)
   Initial   |  99.8     |   89.2          |   0.06
   3 months  |  99.2     |   86.4          |   0.09
   6 months  |  98.6     |   82.1          |   0.14
   Status: PASSING at 6 months accelerated (spec Q=75% — dissolution margin is adequate but narrow)

3. PHOTOSTABILITY — ICH Q1B
   Product is NOT photosensitive — no significant change observed in open-dish and wrapped conditions.
   Al-Al blister provides adequate light protection.

CRITICAL GAPS IN STABILITY PACKAGE:
- All stability data from Catalent production batches ONLY
- No stability data from Lonza site batches — required per ICH Q10 for site change
- Confirmatory batches at Lonza: 0 of 3 required initiated
- 24-month long-term data not yet available (study ongoing — 36-month shelf life claimed)
- Stability data specific to Lonza packaging configuration (blister sealing) not generated
- Particle size stability: d90 not monitored post-initial timepoint (BCS Class II risk)

SHELF LIFE CLAIM: 36 months (based on Catalent data only)

CRITICAL REGULATORY NOTICE — NITROSAMINES:
Valsartan is subject to FDA and EMA nitrosamine guidance (NDMA, NDEA, NMBA).
A Nitrosamine Drug Substance Related Impurity (NDSRI) risk assessment is MANDATORY
for ALL manufacturing site changes, supplier changes, and new equipment introductions.
Receiving site supply chain (API supplier, excipient suppliers, capsule shell supplier)
must each be individually assessed.
NOTE: NDSRI assessment for Thermo Fisher supply chain NOT completed in this package.

API FORM NOTICE:
Valsartan used in this product is partially amorphous (enhanced dissolution — BCS Class II).
Recrystallisation to crystalline form reduces dissolution significantly.
Temperature during processing must NOT exceed 50°C. Humidity must remain < 35% RH.

MANUFACTURING STEPS:

1. API Characterisation (Pre-processing Check)
   - Confirm amorphous content by DSC prior to processing
   - Tg (glass transition temperature): typically 72–78°C for target amorphous fraction
   - NOTE: Fareva uses DSC — Thermo Fisher Swindon site does not have DSC capability;
     amorphous content verification gap must be resolved before manufacture

2. Dispensing
   - Weigh Valsartan API: 40.0 kg
   - Weigh MCC PH-101: 22.0 kg
   - Weigh Crospovidone: 5.0 kg
   - All dispensing under controlled humidity: < 35% RH
   - NOTE: Thermo Fisher facility environmental qualification data not confirmed in this document

3. Dry Granulation (Roller Compaction)
   - Ribbon LOD target: NMT 0.8%
   - Roll pressure: 40–60 bar
   - NOTE: Fareva uses Alexanderwerk WP200; Thermo Fisher uses Gerteis Mini-Pactor;
     equipment equivalence not established — scale-up study required

4. Granule Milling
   - Conical mill, 0.8mm screen
   - Yield: NLT 96%

5. Post-milling Tray Drying
   - Tray drying at 45°C, maximum 24 hours
   - LOD target: NMT 0.8%
   - CRITICAL: Do NOT exceed 50°C — amorphous API begins to recrystallise above this temperature
   - NOTE: Thermo Fisher oven temperature mapping and monitoring protocol not provided

6. Final Blending
   - Add colloidal silicon dioxide: 0.8 kg
   - Add crospovidone (extragranular): 2.0 kg
   - Blend 10 min at 10 rpm
   - Add magnesium stearate: 0.5 kg, blend 5 min
   - Blend uniformity: NLT 5 locations, RSD NMT 2.0%

7. Encapsulation
   - Hard gelatin capsule size 1 (Thermo Fisher proposes ACG Capsules)
   - NOTE: Fareva qualification data generated with Capsugel Coni-Snap capsules;
     ACG capsule shell not evaluated — nitrosamine risk from new supplier not assessed
   - Target fill weight: 320 mg ± 3%
   - Encapsulation humidity: < 35% RH (CRITICAL — capsule shell integrity)
   - NOTE: Capsule fill weight uniformity validation at Thermo Fisher site not yet started

8. Packaging
   - HDPE bottle with induction seal liner
   - Desiccant: 2g silica gel canister
   - NOTE: Induction seal validation not yet completed at Thermo Fisher site
   - Container closure integrity (CCI) testing: not performed on Thermo Fisher packaging components

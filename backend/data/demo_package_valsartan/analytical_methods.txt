ANALYTICAL METHODS PACKAGE — Valsartan Hard Gelatin Capsules 80mg

1. ASSAY — HPLC Method
   Reference: FAR-AM-VAL-001, Rev 3
   Instrument: Shimadzu LC-2030 HPLC
   Column: C8, 150 x 4.6mm, 5μm (Phenomenex Luna C8)
   Mobile phase: 0.1% formic acid in water : acetonitrile (40:60)
   Flow rate: 1.2 mL/min
   Detection: UV at 250nm
   System suitability: RSD NMT 1.0%
   Validation: Fully validated per ICH Q2(R1)
   Method transfer status: COMPLETE

2. DISSOLUTION — USP Apparatus II
   Reference: FAR-AM-VAL-002, Rev 2
   Medium: 900 mL pH 6.8 phosphate buffer (no surfactant)
   Speed: 75 rpm
   Temperature: 37.0 ± 0.5°C
   Timepoint: 60 min; Acceptance: Q = 70%
   Detection: UV at 250nm
   Method transfer status: PARTIAL — basic transfer complete;
   paddle speed sensitivity study (±5 rpm equivalence) not repeated at Thermo Fisher.
   NOTE: Amorphous valsartan dissolution is highly sensitive to paddle speed, medium volume,
   and vessel geometry. Borderline results (Q=70–78%) observed in Fareva data suggest
   method robustness is critical at the receiving site.

3. NITROSAMINE IMPURITIES — LC-MS/MS (CRITICAL REGULATORY METHOD)
   Reference: FAR-AM-VAL-003, Rev 1
   Analytes: NDMA (limit 96 ng/day), NMBA (limit 96 ng/day), NDEA (limit 26.5 ng/day)
   Instrument: Waters ACQUITY UPLC + Xevo TQ-S triple quadrupole MS (Fareva)
   Method: Headspace-GC or LC-MS/MS per USP <1469> and FDA guidance
   Validation: Validated at Fareva (LOQ 5 ng/mL for each nitrosamine)
   Method transfer status: NOT STARTED
   NOTE: CRITICAL REGULATORY REQUIREMENT — FDA requires nitrosamine testing for all valsartan
   products. Thermo Fisher Swindon does not have a validated LC-MS/MS nitrosamine method.
   External CRO engagement required; CRO qualification and method transfer timeline
   not addressed in this package.

4. AMORPHOUS CONTENT — DSC
   Reference: FAR-AM-VAL-004, Rev 1
   Method: Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC)
   Specification: Absence of crystalline melt endotherm at 105–115°C confirms amorphous content
   Validation: Specificity demonstrated vs. crystalline reference standard
   Method transfer status: NOT STARTED
   NOTE: CRITICAL GAP — Thermo Fisher Swindon does not have DSC capability.
   XRPD as alternative is available but semi-quantitative for amorphous fraction.
   Amorphous content directly impacts dissolution — batch release without this test is a regulatory risk.

5. WATER CONTENT — Karl Fischer Titration
   Reference: FAR-AM-VAL-005, Rev 1
   Limit: NMT 1.5%
   Validation: Complete
   Method transfer status: COMPLETE

6. RELATED SUBSTANCES — HPLC Gradient
   Reference: FAR-AM-VAL-006, Rev 2
   Known impurities: Valsartan N-oxide, tetrazole degradant, valsartan dimer
   Limits: Individual known impurity NMT 0.15%; unknown NMT 0.10%; total NMT 0.50%
   Validation: Complete
   Method transfer status: COMPLETE

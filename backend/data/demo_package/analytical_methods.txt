ANALYTICAL METHODS PACKAGE — Metformin HCl Tablets 500mg

1. ASSAY — HPLC Method
   Reference: INN-AM-001, Rev 3
   Instrument: Agilent 1260 HPLC
   Column: C18, 150 x 4.6mm, 5μm
   Mobile phase: 0.1% TFA in water : acetonitrile (85:15)
   Flow rate: 1.0 mL/min
   Detection: UV at 232nm
   Injection volume: 10 μL
   Run time: 10 min
   System suitability: RSD NMT 1.0%, tailing factor NMT 2.0
   Validation status: Fully validated per ICH Q2(R1) — specificity, linearity, accuracy, precision, range
   Method transfer status: PARTIAL — precision and accuracy data transferred; ROBUSTNESS data not yet generated at BioMed CDMO

2. DISSOLUTION — USP Apparatus II
   Reference: INN-AM-002, Rev 2
   Apparatus: USP Type II (paddle)
   Medium: 900 mL 0.1N HCl
   Speed: 75 rpm
   Temperature: 37.0 ± 0.5°C
   Timepoint: 45 min
   Acceptance: Q = 80%
   Analytical method: UV at 232nm
   Method transfer status: NOT COMPLETED — dissolution method transfer validation has not been performed at BioMed CDMO
